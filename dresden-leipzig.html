1st Dresden - Leipzig Day

October 30th, 2024

Welcome to the very first Dresden-Leipzig gathering, where we have short informal talks, seminar presentations and discussion sessions to exchange ideas between the two MPI groups: Algebraic Systems Biology and Nonlinear Algebra.
Join us for collaborative discussions and networking opportunities!
